Production Facilities

State-of-the-art manufacturing capabilities for permanent magnet production

MAGNETRIX SYSTEMS PVT. LTD. operates advanced production facilities equipped with cutting-edge technology for manufacturing high-quality permanent magnets. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing lines ensure precision, consistency, and superior quality in every product we deliver.

We have invested significantly in modern production equipment and automation systems to optimize efficiency and maintain the highest quality standards. Our facilities are designed to handle both large-scale production runs and specialized custom orders with equal excellence.

Advanced Mixing Systems
High-precision alloy mixing and preparation systems ensuring optimal material composition and homogeneity.
  • Automated raw material handling
  • Precision weighing and batching
  • Vacuum melting furnaces
  • Inert atmosphere protection
Powder Processing
State-of-the-art powder metallurgy equipment for creating fine, uniform magnetic powders.
  • Jet milling systems
  • Hydrogen decrepitation units
  • Particle size analyzers
  • Automated powder handling
Pressing & Forming
Advanced isostatic and die pressing systems for precise magnet shaping and density control.
  • Cold isostatic pressing (CIP)
  • Magnetic field orientation
  • Multi-axis pressing machines
  • Automated green body handling
Sintering Furnaces
High-temperature vacuum sintering furnaces with precise temperature and atmosphere control.
  • Vacuum sintering chambers
  • Multi-zone temperature control
  • Programmable heating profiles
  • Rapid cooling systems
Precision Machining
CNC machining centers and grinding equipment for achieving exact dimensional specifications.
  • 5-axis CNC machines
  • Surface and cylindrical grinders
  • Wire EDM cutting systems
  • Automated inspection stations
Surface Treatment
Comprehensive coating and surface protection systems for enhanced corrosion resistance.
  • Electroplating lines
  • Epoxy coating systems
  • Passivation treatments
  • Quality inspection stations
