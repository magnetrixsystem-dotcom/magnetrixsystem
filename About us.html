About Us

Specializing in advanced manufacturing and industrial solutions

MAGNETRIX SYSTEMS PVT. LTD.

MAGNETRIX SYSTEMS PVT. LTD.(Mecwin group) was founded in 2017 with a vision to revolutionize the manufacturing industry. The company covers an area of more than 1,92,000 square meters and a construction area of about 2,00,000 square meters. We specialize in the production and processing of advanced industrial components and solutions.

Our company now has the most advanced production line and technology in the industry. Our products are widely used in aviation, aerospace, automotive, electronics, electro-acoustic, motor, mechanical instruments, medical technology, and other fields. With the rapid development of science and technology, we continue to expand our scope of application.

The company takes "specificity, pragmatism, cooperation, innovation" as the concept, customer-centered approach, with quality and reputation to win the trust of customers. We adhere to technology-oriented development, ensuring product quality excellence, service perfection, and constantly opening up new markets worldwide.
